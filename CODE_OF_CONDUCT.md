# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
At Thrift Shop AI, we are committed to providing a friendly and welcoming environment for everyone. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for participants within the platform and the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code applies to all participants, including contributors, users, and other community members.

## Our Standards
We encourage the following behaviors:

- **Respectful Communication**: Be kind and courteous. We have varied backgrounds and each member has their own unique experiences and perspectives.
- **Constructive Feedback**: Provide feedback to the best of your ability and receive it in good faith.
- **Inclusivity**: Make an effort to include diverse perspectives and embrace differences.

## Unacceptable Behavior
Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or bullying of any kind.
- Discriminatory comments based on race, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or any other characteristic.
- Disruption of discussions or activities.
- Inappropriate comments or jokes about any of the above characteristics.

## Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior
Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated. Participants who engage in unacceptable behavior may be warned, temporarily suspended, or permanently expelled, depending on the severity of the behavior.

## Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to a project maintainer or email [contact@thriftshopai.com](mailto:contact@thriftshopai.com). We will review the situation and take appropriate action.

## Acknowledgment
By participating in this community, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct. Let’s work together to ensure a safe and positive environment for everyone!
